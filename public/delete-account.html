Samvaad - Delete Your Account

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Samvaad app on your device.
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon on the home screen).
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your choice twice when prompted.

What happens after deletion

  • Your account is scheduled for deletion with a 30-day grace period.
  • If you sign back in within 30 days, your account is automatically restored.
  • After 30 days, the following data is permanently deleted:
    • Your profile information (name, preferences, priorities)
    • Assessment answers
    • AI Coach conversation history
    • Your Firebase authentication account
