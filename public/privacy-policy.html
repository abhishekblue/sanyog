Welcome to Vivaahik Samvaad (the "App"), a mobile application designed to assist individuals in the Indian arranged marriage process through self-assessment tools, personalized question generation, and AI-powered coaching. The App is developed and operated by Siddharth Jain, an individual developer operating under the brand name Pristine Blocks ("we", "us", or "our").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, protect, and share your personal information when you use our App. By accessing or using Vivaahik Samvaad, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

This Privacy Policy is published in compliance with the Information Technology Act, 2000, the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011, and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, as applicable.

When you register and use the App, we collect the following personal information:

  • Age range
  • Self-assessment responses (personality traits, lifestyle preferences, family values, relationship priorities, and partner preferences)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, we may automatically collect:

All payment transactions are processed through Google Play Store. We do not collect, store, or process any payment card details, bank account information, or UPI credentials. Google Play handles all billing and payment processing in accordance with its own privacy policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your user account
  • To generate your personality and priority assessment results
  • To generate personalized questions tailored to your assessment profile
  • To power the AI Coach feature and provide contextually relevant guidance
  • To improve and optimize the App experience
  • To communicate with you regarding updates, support, or policy changes
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security threats
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations

4. AI Coach and Data Processing

The AI Coach feature in Vivaahik Samvaad is powered by Google Gemini. When you interact with the AI Coach:

  • Your conversation data is transmitted to Google's Gemini API for processing and response generation.
  • Your AI Coach conversations are stored on our servers to maintain conversation continuity and improve the quality of guidance provided.
  • Conversation data sent to Google Gemini is processed in accordance with Google's API terms of service and data handling policies.
  • We do not use your AI Coach conversations for advertising purposes or share them with third parties beyond what is necessary for the AI processing.

You may request deletion of your AI Coach conversation history at any time by contacting us or using the account deletion feature within the App.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission using HTTPS/TLS protocols
  • Firebase security rules and authentication mechanisms
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized processes only
  • Regular security reviews and updates

While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

  • Safety: To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public.

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services.
